Meet Our Experts

Our guides are not just tour guides. They are scholars, artists, and cultural inheritors who have dedicated their lives to preserving Chinese heritage.

He Yunying

Provincial-level Nüshu Inheritor

Master He has been practicing Nüshu for over 50 years. She learned the script from her grandmother and mother, and has taught more than 1,000 students from around the world. She is dedicated to preserving this unique cultural heritage for future generations.

"Nüshu is not just a writing system. It is the voice of our mothers and grandmothers. It is the story of women's strength and sisterhood."
